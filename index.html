What to do

Listen to all 10 of the Federalist Papers below. For each one, write a short response (about 4–6 sentences) in the box under the player and click Submit response. Your responses save to our class record and appear in the “Class responses” list under each paper.

In each response, try to hit:

  • What is the main argument the author is making?
  • One line, quote, or moment that stood out to you.
  • Your reaction, or a question it raised for you.
  • A connection to today, if you can find one.

Tip: spread these out across the summer — about one a week — instead of saving them for the last days. Audio readings are by VonClegg Classics (public domain text).
